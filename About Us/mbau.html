About Us

The Moon Bakery started as a small bakery inspired by the beauty of the Earth's only natural satellite, the Moon. Our goal is to produce freshly-baked goods for our customers to satisfy their cravings in the best way. We always insist to use the best ingredients like premium butter, fresh eggs and most importantly no added preservatives to bake to ensure every product tastes perfect and healthy.

Our bakers have been always collecting feedback from our customers and try their best to innovate new recipes to provide more choices for our customers and serve them better products. We believe, with our passion, we can create more happy moments for every customer, worker and even their family.
